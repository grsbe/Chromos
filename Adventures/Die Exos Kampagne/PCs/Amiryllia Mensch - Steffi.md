Orb der Tiefen See?
Broken Blood well vial (Wondrous Item, Spellcasting Focus, Common (Requires Attunement By a Sorcerer)
To attune to this vial, you must place a few drops of your blood into it. The vial can't be opened while your attunement to it lasts. If your attunement to the vial ends, the contained blood turns to ash. You can use the vial as a spellcasting focus for your spells while wearing or holding it
In addition, when you roll any Hit Dice to recover hit points while you are carrying the vial, you can regain 2 sorcery points. This property of the vial can't be used again until the next dawn.
